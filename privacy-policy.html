You may set your browser and your mobile settings to block cookies and local storage devices, but if you do so, you may not be able to access the features that polydating.site offers.

polydating.site uses cookies and local storage devices for a number of reasons, including to ensure the security of our site, to provide you with features you have requested and to analyse how members and visitors use polydating.site. polydating.site’s uses of cookies and local shared devices so that we know who you are, what interests you and so that you can do the things online that you want to do, like chat with new people, share your content and buy Super Powers knowing that your data will be secure. Please visit this page regularly so that you are aware of any changes.
